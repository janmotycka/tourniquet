TORQ — Sports tournaments, matches, and trainings for youth coaches.

Are you a PE teacher taking the kids to a school cup? Running a youth football team? Organizing a tournament? TORQ is for you.

⚡ SIMPLE MODE (free, unlimited)
- Create a match in 30 seconds — opponent, roster, play
- Tap scores directly on phone, timer runs automatically
- Send results to parents via WhatsApp (one tap)
- Share image on Instagram or Facebook
- Save your team and reuse it next match
- Create tournaments with 4-16 teams, print bracket + schedule

🏟️ ADVANCED MODE (club)
- Shared workspace with assistant coaches
- Multi-trainer lock — both edit live without conflicts
- Seasonal player statistics (goals, assists, cards, playing time)
- Player ratings after match (5 attributes)
- FA-format match report PDF (Czech FA standard)
- Cross-team pairing — opponent joins via PIN, both record together

🎾 THREE SPORTS
- ⚽ Football (formats 3+1 to 11+1, official + amateur)
- 🎾 Tennis (singles + doubles)
- 🏑 Floorball (quick match + tournament)

🎬 LIVE SHARING
- Spectators watch scores in real time via QR code or link
- No registration for spectators
- Push notifications on goals (optional)

💚 FREE FOREVER
- Simple mode: no limits
- Advanced: 10 matches + 5 trainings + 1 tournament free
- Premium €3.99 / month — unlimited, but not required

LOVED BY:
- PE teachers
- Youth team coaches (U6-U19)
